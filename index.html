I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Information Technology, Alpen-Adria-Universität, Klagenfurt, Austria, where I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science. I currently focus on the adaptive-bitrate (ABR) strategies of the player component in video streaming in the Christian-Doppler Laboratory ATHENA, led by Prof. Christian Timmerer. I received my master degree in ICT Engineering and my bachelor degree in Information Engineering from the Department of Information Engineering, University of Padua, Italy. My research interests encompass Video Streaming, Multimedia Networking, Machine and Deep Learning, QoS and QoE Improvement.
